Solafact 5060
‍

Product Introduction

The Solafact 5060, a lightweight Fourier Transform Toxic Gas Analyzer, primarily employs Fourier-transform infrared spectroscopy technology. It combines a highly compact, corrosion-resistant, and highly reliable 3-meter micro-path absorption cell. This device is capable of fast and precise detection of various chemical warfare agents (CWA) and industrial toxic hazardous gases (TIC) at ppm levels.
‍
The instrument features a spatially compact Michelson interferometer, independently developed by our company, offering significant technical advantages. It supports versatile detection, low detection limits, lightweight design, and low energy consumption. It can be deployed on small unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), robots, and other mobile platforms, enabling quick measurements and alerts during movement.
‍
The Solafact 5060 finds wide applications in national security, public security forces, emergency response, field reconnaissance, chemical industrial zones, and pollution source monitoring, among other areas.

Features

‍
1. Highly Integrated Internal Optics and Hardware: The Solafact 5060 boasts a highly integrated design, making it the first of its kind in the domestic market. This integration enhances its technical capabilities.

2. Compact, Lightweight, and Powerful: With its small form factor and powerful functionality, the device can be used in various scenarios, such as individual carrying, UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) deployment, and unmanned robots.

3. Wide Range of Detectable Gases: The instrument can detect over forty different chemical warfare agents and toxic hazardous gases at concentrations greater than 5 ppm. Additionally, it can be expanded to accommodate user-specific requirements.

4. Low Power Consumption: The device operates with low power consumption and is equipped with a built-in 18650 lithium battery, offering over 4hours of continuous operation at room temperature.

5. High Reliability: Utilizing professional multi-component gas qualitative and quantitative algorithms, the instrument is suitable for detecting and analyzing mixed gases in various complex environments.

‍

The product is the first of its kind in the domestic market, positioned between ion mobility spectrometry and portable mass spectrometry.
