Product Introduction

SOLAFACT 820 is a nuclear detection UAV. The flight platform is DJI's industry-level Matrice M300 RTK UAV. SiPM's nuclear detection technology can detect trace chemical agents, industrial toxic and harmful gases and radioactive pollution online, quickly and accurately.The results of nuclear chemical reconnaissance and detection can be displayed in real time on the UAV control terminal through transparent transmission. The professional visual analysis software can be seamlessly connected with nuclear chemical reconnaissance and inspection drones to achieve second-level response and draw nuclear and chemical pollution in real time. The three-dimensional pollution concentration distribution in the area is clear at a glance, and the pollution source can be quickly identified.
